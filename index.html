About Us

Smith & Anvil LLC is a woman-owned, small business dedicated to delivering reliable electronic products and solutions. We support the United States Military, Federal, State, & Local government agencies, and commercial clients. We do so with a commitment to patriotism, faith, discipline, and personal responsibility. Our mission is to provide trusted technology today while forging the future through research and development.

Core Competencies

  • Wholesale distribution of electronics and IT hardware
  • Support for government and defense procurement requirements
  • Supply chain efficiency and responsive customer service
  • Future-focused development in electronics manufacturing and R&D

Differentiators

  • Small business agility with scalable distribution capacity
  • Commitment to supporting U.S. military and defense readiness
  • Family-driven values: integrity, responsibility, and service
  • Focused on innovation and long-term R&D growth
